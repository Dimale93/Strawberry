Joly Advantages

    Advantages:

  • Higher than average productivity (740-900 g per bush);

  • Size and uniformity of berries, a large percentage of fruits of class I quality;

  • Resistance to leaf diseases and root rot;

  • Drought resistance;

  • Independence of the quality and size of berries from humidity;

  • Ease of propagation and good survival of seedlings;

  • Good adaptation to the conditions of different regions;

  • Varieties do not need complex agricultural technology;

  • Good transportability - berries do not run, withstand prolonged transport and storage.
