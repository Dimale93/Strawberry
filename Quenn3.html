Queen Elisa Advantages

    Advantages:

  • Early epoch of maturation;

  • The root is lush and very productive;

  • It is tolerant to anthracnose;

  • It is tolerant to leaf spot;

  • The skin of the fruit is firm, reddish-glossy in color;

  • The flesh of the fruit is tasty and aromatic;

  • Drought resistant;

  • It is suitable for growing in the open field as well as in greenhouses;
