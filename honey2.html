Honey Characteristics

Shrubs dense, erect and strong, to form a strong root system. Leaves complex consisting of 3 leaves handles, grows to a length of 23 cm. Each cone grows leaves 11-13. This variety growing season begins in mid-March.

Strawberry blooms in early May, about 15 days.Fruits ripen at the same time depending on the planting in the region, starting from 15-25 May.In the application of greenhouses or agrofiber can get the first harvest 2 weeks earlier.


The red fruit is of medium density, has a juicy and sweet-sour taste. The berries of the first harvest are large, weighing 35-40 g. In the period of mass harvest the fruits are smaller but much sweeter.



Yields in the "Honey" strawberry is very high: for single-landing time up to 146 kg / ha, while Multitape - 126 kg / ha. Average 400-500 g from the bush. Growing strawberries Honey is not an easy task, it requires a lot of time and effort. Very often people simply do not have time to properly monitor the plants planted. Planting strawberries on agrofiber greatly facilitates work and increases productivity.
