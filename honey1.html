Honey Description

Honey strawberry - the fruit of hard work of breeders from America. Berry was grown in the 80s of last century. Its first harvest was obtained near the town of Honoai, which determined the name.

The American variety Holiday was included in the creation of the variety, and an early variety from the English selection, created by David Simpson, Vibrant.


With an area of ​​one square meter, you can collect an average of one kilogram of berries. They Honey are an interesting feature: ascorbic acid is found in them in large quantities, about 67%.

The culture has strong fluffy shrubs. The plant has few leaves, up to 20 cm in size, and mild wrinkles are visible on the dark green surface. The roots are very strong, have great vitality. The mustache is long. Peduncles strong, 10-15 inflorescences are formed on each plant. A prerequisite for normal growth and high yield is the choice of a flat, elevated and lighted area. On steep slopes and in the lowlands, where precipitation and low air temperatures accumulate, it is better not to plant honey strawberries. It will ripen badly here, the roots will start to rot.
