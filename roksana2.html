Roxana Characteristics

Roxana has very large, uniform, attractive fruits, elongated cone shape, medium consistency, bright red color, good taste and high commercial value.

It is very profitable in autumn production and in hydroponic production in bags. The average yield per hedge in good conditions is 1 to 1.2 kg. The average weight of the fruit is 26-28 g and the penetration resistance is 30-33 g / cm2, but not as in Alba.


Strawberry fruits ripen en masse, evenly. Picking berries is not difficult due to good availability. Since the strawberry "Roxana", according to gardeners, refers to the middle late varieties, it is suitable for late harvest.

Many summer residents grow it in such a way that the peak of fruiting comes in the fall. Lowering the ambient temperature and poor lighting do not affect the plant yield, taste and appearance of aromatic berries.

It grows well at higher altitudes and lower day and night temperatures. Even in conditions of limited irrigation, it gives high yields of up to 1 kg per hedge.
