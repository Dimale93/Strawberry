Joly Description

Strawberry Jolie is not so widespread in Russia, although in the homeland of Italy this variety is considered one of the best. European farmers grow this strawberry for commercial purposes because of its beautiful shape, large size, pleasant taste and fairly high yield.

Jolie strawberries were obtained by Italian growers from the CIV consortium for crossing varieties T2-6 and A20-17 (these varieties also participated in the production of strawberries Cleri and Darselect). The work on the selection to get Jolie has been carried out since 2004, and already in 2005-2010. It has been tested in European fields.


This garden strawberry was developed as a fruitful variety with beautiful, uniform berries that are perfect for sale.

Not too lush, but a solid plant, upright with high productivity, resistant roots and leaves. The flower is rich in pollen, it is formed under the leaves, the fruit is abbreviated conical shape of bright red color, sweet taste and fine aroma.
