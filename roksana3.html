Roxana Advantages

    Advantages:

  • Genetic resistance to powdery mildew;

  • Even ripening of berries;

  • Good approach to fruit collection;

  • Taste: balanced sweet-sour taste with little bitterness and pronounced aroma.

  • Transportability and maintenance of fruit quality;

  • Suitable for commercial cultivation;

  • When the early varieties have finished bearing fruit, Roxana delights the gardeners with the first harvest;

  • By lowering air and soil temperatures, lighting does not affect Roxanne features;

  • The ability of the ripe fruit to hang weekly on the bush without losing the elasticity of the berries and deteriorating taste.
