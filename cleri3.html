Clery Advantages

    Advantages:

  • High resistance of berries to transport and long shelf life (up to 5 days);

  • Universality of berry use (for any culinary processing and freezing);

  • It can be used in diseases of the gastrointestinal tract and high acidity, because they do not contain acid;

  • Good winter hardiness and average drought tolerance;

  • Unpretentious to soil composition;

  • Good resistance to diseases of the root system, medium - to brown and white spots.

  • It adapts well to different cultivation systems;

  • The flesh of the fruit is of good quality;

  • Expressed aromas.
