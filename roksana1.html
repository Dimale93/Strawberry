Roxana Description

One of the most productive varieties of strawberries, solid fruits, intended for sale in distant markets. It has a very lush plant, high resistance to root diseases.

Originals of the variety - New Fruits growers from Italy. The cultivation and testing of Roxanne was carried out in the fields of a company located near Cesena. Since 2001, strawberries can be bought in the retail network.


Mustache formation is moderate - nutrients are spent on the formation and ripening of berries. Initially, the berries have a conical shape, which is characterized by a split with intensive growth and the formation of small tubercles at the top of the fruit.

In erect plants elongated stalks. In the first season, during flowering blooms 1 or 2 flowers, which positively affects the size of the fruit. Strawberries are larger compared to the following harvest seasons. It is not difficult to grow Roxanne on a personal plot, it is enough to observe agricultural technology and implement the prevention of pests and infections. According to the description, Roxana strawberry belongs to the middle late varieties in terms of ripening time.
