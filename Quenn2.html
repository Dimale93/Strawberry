Quenn Elisa Characteristics

It ripens early, at the end of the first decade of May. She has a good birth rate. In the open field, the average yield per bokor is one kilogram, or 800 grams in a greenhouse.

The fruit is large, regular conical shape, light to very red, firm skin and flesh. It has an excellent, mostly sweet taste and pronounced aroma.

Suitable for continental areas. Bokor is lush and very productive. It is tolerant to anthracnose and leaf spot.

Compared to other varieties, its advantages are the early epoch of ripening, pronounced firmness and attractiveness of the fruit, pleasant taste and aroma.
